Hi - I'm Rohil, a junior at MIT studying Computer Science and Mathematics. My interests lie in applying state-of-the-art research to solve practical problems, and in establishing guarantees and failure cases for these techniques. Through this, I hope to avoid hype and instead create rigorous, well-understood solutions.
In the past, I've done research in political science and aerospace groups, and worked at payments and cryptocurrency companies. This summer, I will be working at Celect where I will be studying the prediction of fashion trends, enabling retailers to better optimize their inventory. Starting next fall, I will be studying confidence metrics for neural networks in Prof. Daniela Rus' autonomous driving lab.
